Inky's World Famous Pasta

Final Product

Rotini Pasta with Parmesan, Baby Spinach, Red Sauce, and Spices. The easiest, most filling, and flavorful pasta ever!

Ingredients

  • Bowtie Pasta
  • 1 Cup Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 Cup of Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (Vegan, if you like)
  • Marinara Sauce
  • A Generous Pinch of Salt
  • Freshly Cracked Black Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 Teasppon Onion Powder
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. In a medium pot, bring 4 cups of cold water to a rolling boil. While filling your pot with water add 2 tablespoons of salt.
  2. Once boiling, add your pasta and cook for the lower amount of time listed on the box (usually 10 minutes). Tip: Stir for the first 2 minutes of cooking time to keep noodles from sticking to each other and the pot.
  3. Check the done-ness of the pasta ~2 minutes from the timer going off. It should be aldente, or slightly chewy.
  4. Take a mug or cup and scoop up some of the pasta water, this will be added to the sauce for a boost of flavor.
  5. Strain the pasta and shake to remove excess water.
  6. Return the now-empty pot to the stovetop, turn the heat down to medium, and add some oil.
  7. Once the oil is heated (~1 minute) add your Baby Spinach. This will cook fast, have the marinara sauce ready.
  8. After the Spinach is wilted, add the sauce and stir.
  9. Add your noodles back to the pot along with your spices and cheese.
  10. It is ready when the cheese has melt and the sauce is bubbling and steaming.
  11. Finally, plate your pasta and garnish with a single, uncooked spinach leaf on top.
