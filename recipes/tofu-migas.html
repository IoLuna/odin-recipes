Tofu Migas

Final Product

This recipe was stolen from the Vegan Stoner Cookbook. Here is a link to their website, go show them some love: The Vegan Stoner

Ingredients

  • 4 Medium Torillas
  • 1/2 Small Red Onion
  • 1/2 Block Firm Tofu
  • 1 Can of Black Beans
  • 1 Can of Diced Green Chiles
  • Green Onions
  • Salsa Verde
  • A Pinch of Salt
  • Freshly Cracked Black Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 Teasppon Onion Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Cumin
  • A Dash of Cayenne Pepper
  • A Dash of Smoked Peprika
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Chop red onion and heat in oiled pan.
  2. Stack and slice torillas into 1/2" stripes.
  3. Mix strips into pan with a half cup of salsa verde and can of Diced Green Chiles.
  4. In separate bowl, mash the half block of tofu with a fork, mix with a handful of nutritional yeast and other spices.
  5. Heat a drained can of black beans in a separate pan.
  6. Serve the migas next to beans and top with chopped green onions.
